and Luau's positive modulo ((negative % 5) + 2) pins every owned item
on a flash frame forever. Fixed by wiping item_gettime/stats/
faceanimtime/cshift percents/dmg_time/punchangle/time/mtime0/oldtime
in parseServerInfo, matching the C memset.
