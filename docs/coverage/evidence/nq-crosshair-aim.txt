nq-crosshair-aim.jpg — SCR_DrawCrosshair placement fix (playtest report:
"the crosshair is not actually the location that guns shoot").

CAPTURE CONTEXT: Studio Play, NQ boot, start map, deathmatch 0.
Produced by tools/verify_crosshair.luau PART 3: rocket launcher fired at
a wall ~140qu ahead, server console "pause" issued 0.32s after the
trigger — BecomeExplosion is a server think chain, so the s_explod
fireball froze mid-animation for an unhurried capture. The '+' overlaps
the fireball's upper edge; the small gap is the authentic 6qu below-eye
rocket spawn line (~29px at that range), not misalignment.

ROOT CAUSE: C draws the '+' at the vrect centre, which in WinQuake IS
the projection centre (the 3d view is squeezed above the sbar). Our
Roblox camera cannot shift its projection centre off the window centre
(qcoords.vrect comment), so the vrect-centre emulation put the glyph
48px (half the 2x-scaled sbar) above the real aim line — every shot
landed below the crosshair.

NUMERIC CHECKS (verify_crosshair.luau PARTS 1-2, recorded this session):
  PART 1 glyph centre vs projection centre: delta=(0.0, 0.0)
    (GetGuiInset-corrected; AbsolutePosition is inset-relative even on
    an IgnoreGuiInset gui)
  PART 2 tracked missile terminal position projected to screen:
    dist=673qu screen delta=(-14.0, 18.8) px — within one 16px glyph;
    ~6px of the Y residue is the 6qu spawn offset, the rest the
    missile mesh centre vs entity origin.
